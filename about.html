Bio

I am a research engineer specializing in nano-/microfabrication and 2D materials, with a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from Stevens Institute of Technology. My work involves cutting-edge research into the synthesis and application of materials like graphene and transition metal dichalcogenides. Currently, I lead the development of the Microfabrication Innovation Center at NJIT.
